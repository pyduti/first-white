Terms of Service

These terms explain how our travel planning service works and what you can expect when you contact domain about a trip.

Last updated: 1 May 2026

1. What we do

domain helps travellers plan tailored journeys across Great Britain. Our service may include route ideas, destination suggestions, timing guidance, local travel insight, and communication about possible travel arrangements.

2. Enquiry-based service

Our website is designed for enquiries. When you submit a form, we review the information you provide and may contact you to better understand your preferred dates, group size, pace, interests, and destination choices.

3. No automatic booking

Sending a form does not automatically reserve a tour, guide, hotel, transport service, activity, or other travel arrangement. Any confirmed arrangement must be agreed separately in writing.

4. Tailored planning

Every trip request is different. We may suggest a route, travel style, destination combination, or practical timing based on the details you provide. Suggestions are not fixed guarantees unless confirmed in writing as part of an agreed arrangement.

5. Communication

We normally communicate by email or phone using the details you provide. Please make sure your contact information is accurate so we can respond properly.

6. Practical travel limits

Travel planning depends on real-world conditions. Weather, road closures, seasonal opening hours, supplier availability, transport changes, and local events may affect routes or recommendations.

7. Third-party services

Some travel experiences may involve independent third parties. These can include accommodation providers, transport companies, guides, attractions, restaurants, or activity providers. They may have their own terms, prices, rules, and cancellation conditions.

8. Support

We aim to provide helpful and responsive support before, during, and after your journey. Unless a specific support arrangement is confirmed in writing, we do not present the website as an emergency response service.

9. Your role in planning

Good travel planning depends on clear information. Please tell us about any important requirements early, including mobility needs, dietary preferences, preferred travel pace, budget expectations, or accessibility questions.

10. Website content

Our articles, service pages, and destination descriptions are written to help visitors understand possible travel experiences. They are not a substitute for official travel, safety, medical, legal, or immigration advice.

11. Relationship with other policies

These Terms of Service should be read together with our Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policy, and Cookie Policy.

12. Contact

Questions about our service can be sent to info@domain.com.
