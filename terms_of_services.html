5.1 Cancellation by You

If you wish to cancel your booking, you must notify us in writing to info@domain.com. Cancellation charges apply from the date we receive your written notice, as follows:

We strongly recommend you obtain comprehensive travel insurance that covers cancellation costs at the time of booking.
