Our Story

WanderBritain was founded in 2009 by a group of passionate British travellers who believed that the best way to see Great Britain was through the eyes of those who truly know it. Frustrated with generic tours that rushed visitors past the real Britain, we set out to build something different.

Over 15 years later, we have guided more than 12,000 travellers through cobblestone market towns, mist-covered Scottish glens, and the wild coasts of Wales. Our philosophy remains the same: slow down, dig deeper, and travel authentically.

Based in Bristol, we work with a hand-picked network of local guides, family-run accommodation, and independent eateries across all four nations — ensuring every pound you spend supports communities, not corporations.
