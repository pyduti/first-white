Privacy Policy

This page explains how domain collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our website or contact us about travel services.

Last updated: 1 May 2026

1. Who we are

domain is a travel agency based at 14 Thornbury Lane, Bristol, BS1 4NX, United Kingdom. We create tailored travel experiences across England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland.

You can contact us by email at info@domain.com or by phone at +44 20 7946 0391.

2. Information we collect

We may collect information that you choose to send to us through our enquiry or contact forms, including:

  • your name;
  • your email address;
  • your phone number, if you provide it;
  • your preferred destination or travel region;
  • your message, travel dates, group size, interests, and other enquiry details;
  • basic technical information needed to keep the website secure and working properly.

3. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • respond to your enquiry;
  • prepare travel suggestions or follow-up questions;
  • communicate with you about your requested trip;
  • maintain website security and prevent spam or misuse;
  • understand how visitors use our website, where analytics tools are enabled;
  • meet legal, accounting, or regulatory obligations where they apply.

4. Legal basis for using personal information

We process enquiry information because it is necessary to respond to your request before entering into any travel arrangement. We may also process information for our legitimate interests, such as website security, service improvement, and basic business administration. Where consent is required, such as for certain cookies or marketing activity, we rely on your consent.

5. Cookies, analytics, and advertising tools

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to make the site work properly, protect forms from misuse, understand visitor activity, and measure advertising performance. These technologies may include cookies, pixels, browser identifiers, IP addresses, or similar technical information.

If we use tools such as Google Ads, Google Analytics, or similar services, those providers may process limited technical data to help us understand how visitors reach and interact with our website. You can read more about cookies in our Cookie Policy.

6. Sharing information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when reasonably necessary, including with:

  • trusted service providers who help us operate the website or manage enquiries;
  • travel-related providers where needed to prepare or discuss a requested itinerary;
  • professional advisers, if required for legal or accounting purposes;
  • public authorities, if required by law.

7. How long we keep information

We keep enquiry information only for as long as reasonably needed to respond to your request, manage follow-up communication, maintain business records, or meet legal obligations. If your enquiry does not lead to an ongoing arrangement, we aim to keep the information no longer than necessary.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the information you send to us. No website or email transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure, so please avoid sending highly sensitive personal information through the contact forms.

9. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live and which laws apply, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the use of your personal information. You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To make a privacy request, contact us at info@domain.com.

10. Children

Our website is intended for adults making travel enquiries. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
