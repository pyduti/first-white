Cookie Policy

This page explains how domain may use cookies and similar technologies to keep the website working, improve user experience, and understand visitor activity.

Last updated: 1 May 2026

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember basic information, support security, measure performance, and understand how visitors interact with pages.

2. Similar technologies

We may also use similar technologies such as pixels, tags, scripts, local storage, IP addresses, browser identifiers, or device information. In this policy, we refer to these together as cookies.

3. Essential cookies

Essential cookies help the website function properly. They may support basic navigation, form protection, spam prevention, security checks, and session handling. These cookies are necessary for the site to work as expected.

4. Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use the website, such as which pages are viewed, how long visitors stay, and how people move between pages. This helps us improve content, navigation, and user experience.

5. Advertising and measurement cookies

If we run online advertising, we may use advertising or measurement cookies to understand whether ads are useful, how visitors arrive on the website, and whether an enquiry is submitted after a visit. These cookies may be set by third-party providers, including advertising or analytics platforms.

These tools may process technical information such as cookie IDs, browser data, approximate location, IP address, device type, page views, and interaction events. We do not use these tools to collect highly sensitive personal information through the website.

6. Form and security cookies

Our enquiry forms may use security-related checks to help prevent spam, automated submissions, or misuse. These checks are designed to protect both visitors and the website.

7. Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or limit cookies. If you block all cookies, some parts of the website may not work as intended.

8. Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third-party services that help us operate, secure, measure, or promote the website. These providers process information according to their own privacy and cookie policies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if our website, tools, or legal obligations change. The latest version will always be available on this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, contact us at info@domain.com.
