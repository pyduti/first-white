Our Story

domain was created for travellers who want to see Great Britain with more context, more flexibility, and more time to enjoy the places that make each journey memorable.

Built around real travel, not rushed checklists

Founded in 2009 by a group of passionate British travellers, domain began with a simple idea: the best way to experience Great Britain is through thoughtful planning, local insight, and enough time to enjoy the journey properly.

We saw that many visitors were being moved quickly from one famous stop to another, often missing the details that give a place its character. Our goal was to create travel experiences that feel personal, practical, and connected to the places they pass through.

What makes our approach different

We focus on journeys across England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland that balance iconic sights with quieter local experiences. That might mean a scenic Highland route, a slower day through Cotswold villages, time on the Welsh coast, or a city break shaped around food, history, and neighbourhood character.

Local knowledge matters

A route can look simple on a map and still feel very different on the ground. Weather, road conditions, seasonal opening hours, walking distances, and local events can all shape the quality of a trip. Our planning process takes these details seriously.

Travel at the right pace

We believe a well-planned trip should leave room to breathe. Some travellers want active days and early starts. Others prefer gentle routes, longer meals, shorter walks, or more time for photography. A good itinerary should reflect the people taking the journey.

Supporting independent places

Based in Bristol, we work with a network of local guides, family-run accommodation, independent eateries, and regional providers where suitable. The aim is to help travellers experience more of the real character of each destination while keeping the journey comfortable and well organised.

Honest planning from the beginning

We do not believe in overloading a schedule or promising that every day will be perfect. Good travel planning means being clear about distances, seasons, weather, pace, and expectations. That honesty helps create smoother, more enjoyable journeys.

Ready to plan your journey?

Tell us what kind of Great Britain experience you have in mind, and we will help shape a route around your interests, timing, comfort, and travel style.
