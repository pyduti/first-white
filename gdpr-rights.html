Your Data Protection Rights

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have significant rights over how your personal data is collected, stored, and used. WanderBritain is committed to upholding these rights fully and transparently.

Response timeframe: WanderBritain will respond to all valid data subject requests within 30 calendar days of receipt. In complex cases, this period may be extended by a further two months, and you will be informed of any extension within the initial 30-day window.

Your Rights at a Glance

The UK GDPR grants you the following rights with respect to your personal data held by WanderBritain. You may exercise any of these rights at any time, free of charge, unless your request is manifestly unfounded or excessive.

Right of Access

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, along with information about how and why we process it. This is commonly known as a Subject Access Request (SAR). We will provide this information in a commonly used electronic format.

Article 15 UK GDPR

Right to Rectification

If you believe any personal data we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request that it be corrected or completed without undue delay. We will notify any third parties with whom we have shared the data of any rectification where reasonably practicable.

Article 16 UK GDPR

Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten")

You may ask us to delete your personal data where it is no longer necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, where you have withdrawn consent, or where processing is otherwise unlawful. This right is not absolute — we may be required to retain certain data to comply with legal obligations or to establish or defend legal claims.

Article 17 UK GDPR

Right to Restrict Processing

In certain circumstances, you may ask us to suspend processing of your personal data — for example, while you contest its accuracy or while you exercise your right to object. Where processing is restricted, we may still store your data but will not actively use it unless you provide consent or it is needed to establish legal claims.

Article 18 UK GDPR

Right to Data Portability

Where we process your data based on your consent or a contract with you, and the processing is carried out by automated means, you have the right to receive a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format (such as CSV or JSON), and to have it transmitted to another controller where technically feasible.

Article 20 UK GDPR

Right to Object

You have the right to object at any time to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes, including profiling related to direct marketing. You may also object to processing carried out on the grounds of legitimate interests, unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds that override your interests, rights, and freedoms.

Article 21 UK GDPR

Right to Withdraw Consent

Where we rely on your consent as the legal basis for processing your personal data — for example, for marketing communications or optional cookies — you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal will not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before you withdraw consent.

You may withdraw consent by:

  • Clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any marketing email we send you
  • Adjusting your cookie preferences via our Cookie Policy page
  • Contacting us directly at info@domain.com

Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making

You have the right not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing — including profiling — that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you. WanderBritain does not currently engage in fully automated decision-making of this nature. If this changes, we will update this page and notify affected individuals accordingly.

What Happens After You Submit a Request

  1. 1
    Acknowledgement — We will acknowledge receipt of your request within 5 working days and may ask you to verify your identity if we are unable to confirm who you are from the information provided.
  2. 2
    Verification — To protect your data and prevent unauthorised disclosure, we will verify your identity before processing your request. We may ask for a copy of a government-issued ID or other reasonable proof.
  3. 3
    Processing — Once verified, we will process your request and fulfil it within 30 calendar days. For complex requests, we may extend this by up to two further months and will notify you promptly.
  4. 4
    Response — We will send our response securely and, where appropriate, provide any data in a portable electronic format. All responses are provided free of charge unless requests are manifestly unfounded or excessive.

Right to Lodge a Complaint with a Supervisory Authority

If you believe that WanderBritain has not handled your personal data in accordance with the UK GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK's data protection supervisory authority:

Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
  • Website: ico.org.uk
  • Helpline: 0303 123 1113 (Monday to Friday, 9:00–17:00)
  • Postal Address: Information Commissioner's Office, Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow, Cheshire, SK9 5AF

We would, however, appreciate the opportunity to address your concerns directly before you approach the ICO. Please contact us first so we can try to resolve your concerns promptly.

Compliance with ePrivacy Regulations

In addition to the UK GDPR, WanderBritain complies with the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR), which sit alongside the UK GDPR and provide specific rules about electronic marketing, cookies, and confidentiality of electronic communications. Our use of cookies and similar technologies is described in our Cookie Policy.

We will never send you unsolicited electronic marketing without your prior consent, and you may opt out of any marketing communications at any time by contacting us at info@domain.com or by using the unsubscribe mechanism included in every marketing message.
